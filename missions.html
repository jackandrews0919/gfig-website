Mission Brief
✈ CLASS E — OPERATIONAL VALIDATION PLATFORM
This mission requires an A319, B737-700, or B727-200 operating as GFIG OPS XX. Do NOT use King Air or other calibration aircraft. Fly at realistic airline speeds. All operations must reflect standard airline procedures.
🔍 CLASS F — SURVEILLANCE OPERATION
This mission requires the Britten-Norman Spylander IS-3T operating as GFIG SPY XX. UK & European airspace only. Maintain low-altitude patrol profile. Coordinate with UK partner agency prior to departure. Do NOT use jet or calibration aircraft.
🚁 CLASS G — AERIAL MEDIA (HELICOPTER)
This mission requires an Airbus H125 or Bell 206B JetRanger operating as GFIG MEDIA XX. Fixed-wing aircraft are not permitted. Observe all low-altitude airspace restrictions and media corridor clearances.
🛸 CLASS H — UAS / DRONE SURVEY
This mission is a partner agency UAS operation. Use Schiebel Camcopter S-100 or DJI Matrice 350 RTK as GFIG UAS XX. File UAS flight plan with the relevant authority (NavCan / NATS / EASA) before departure. Manned aircraft are not permitted for Class H.
📋 After claiming, your mission will appear under "My Active Missions" on the Dashboard. File your report within 24 hours of completion.
