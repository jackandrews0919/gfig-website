Process

How GFIG Works

From joining our network to completing your first inspection mission — structured, operationally rigorous, and rewarding.

01

Join Discord & Register

Join the GFIG Discord server. Login to the website with your Discord account — your inspector profile is created automatically with a unique callsign.

02

Select & Fly Mission

Browse the Mission Board. Pick an available inspection flight, receive your full mission brief, and take to the skies following your assigned callsign and procedures.

03

Report, Rank & Earn

Submit your inspection report — PASS, MONITOR, or FAIL. Results post to Discord automatically. Earn points, advance through inspector ranks, unlock certifications.

Capabilities

What We Inspect

GFIG conducts a full spectrum of virtual flight inspection operations across all major simulator networks and regions.

📡

Navaid Calibration

ILS, VOR, NDB and DME flight checks. Verify signal integrity, glide slope geometry, and localizer alignment across the network.

✈

Procedure Validation

SID, STAR and approach procedure validation missions. Ensure published procedures are flyable, obstacle-clear, and accurate.

🗺

Airport Surveys

Runway surveying, taxiway geometry, obstacle clearance checks, and full airport data validation inspection missions.

📶

ATC Coordination

Full integration with virtual ATC. Inspect approach sectors, coordinate NOTAMs, validate frequencies, and file priority reports.

🎓

Training & Checkrides

Structured training courses, knowledge tests, and practical checkride system to certify inspectors across multiple specializations.

📊

Data Reporting

Comprehensive PASS / MONITOR / FAIL reporting system. All results automatically posted to Discord and tracked in your pilot profile.

Fleet

Global Inspection Fleet

A single unified fleet modelled after real-world flight inspection aircraft. Inspectors transit aircraft to any destination worldwide — from Europe to Asia-Pacific, the Americas to the Middle East — operating as GFIG on VATSIM.

🌍
Worldwide Operations
No region restrictions. Active missions in Europe, North America, Asia-Pacific, Middle East, Africa, Latin America, and beyond.
🛫
Transit Deployments
Aircraft ferry from staging bases via multi-leg transit routes to any inspection site globally. Log the transit flight and the inspection together.
📡
Callsign: GFIG
